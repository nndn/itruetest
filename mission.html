Mission
  Encourage young urologists to ideate and innovate various interventional modalities which can help in development of economical, viable and affordable technology, and make it available to others.
  i-TRUE aims to foster the highest standards in training and research globally to facilitate the comprehensive development of next generation methods, procedures and technologies in the field of Urology.
Vision
  To perform collaborative and multi centre studies with the ultimate goal of improving the care provided to patients with high quality research and interventions.
