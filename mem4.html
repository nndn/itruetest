Er. Nithesh Naik
B.Tech, M.Tech (Design Engineering)
Assistant Professor
Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT)
Manipal Academy of Higher Education
Manipal, India
He is 4+ years of Industry experience and 5+ years of academic experience. He was working with reputed organizations like ETA Engineering Private Limited (ETA Ascon Group), Honeywell Technologies, Tata Consultancy Services. He is currently on academic role with Department of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering, Manipal Institute of Technology, India.
Nithesh is an entrepreneur and has begun his journey as Chief Technical Officer at his Startup Company “Curiouz TechLab Private Limited” working towards excellence in the medical device space, and believe in pursuing business through innovation and technology. At Curiouz, he has developed a mobile application for urologists “Urostentz” for stent and symptom tracking available on Android Play Store and Apple Store. Also, developed patented surgical tool “ZEDD Scissors” and “Rector Scissors” for En-bloc excision of bladder tumours.
Mr. Nithesh has published 41+ research articles in International Journals of repute and has keen interests in medical innovations. He has received award of South India’s most exciting young teacher in the year 2018 from Indian Express for his achievements in research and innovations.
He has applied for Indian and International Patents for his innovations on “X-RAY Film Holder: KID-A-RAY” filed at the Indian Designs Office on 07th June 2018 as a complete application 306405 “An Automatic Light Positioning and Focusing System” filed at the Indian Patent Office on 26th February 2020 as a complete application 202041008172 “Device for Inspecting Dimensions of a Tool Holder” filed at the Indian Designs Office on 03rd October 2019 as a complete application 201941013525 “Universal Self Aligning Automated Gear Hobbing Fixture” filed at the Indian Patent Office on 08th April 2019 as a complete application 201941014152 "A Scissors-Needle System for Intra-Cavitary Hydro-Dissection and Excision of Tissues” filed at the Indian Patent Office as complete application TEMP/E- 1/53807/2019- CHE and international PCT/IB2020/050865
