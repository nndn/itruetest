WELCOME TO i‑TRUE
    The International Training and Research in Uro-Oncology and Endourology Association (i‑TRUE) represents researchers in Urological care globally. It is a dynamic organization with a focus on importance of Training and Research in Uro-Oncology and Endourology for Residents and consultant Urologists.
It is a non-profitable, registered group of international faculties formed to help conduct research, innovate and make scientific progress in Uro-Oncology and Endourology.
i-TRUE aims to foster the highest standards in training and research globally to facilitate the holistic development of next generation methods, procedures and technologies in the field of Urology.
