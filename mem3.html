Dr. BM Zeeshan Hameed
MBBS, MS, MCh (Urology)
Associate Professor of Urology,
Chief-Innovation Center,
Kasturba Medical College,
Manipal Academy of Higher Education,
Manipal, India
He is one of the leading doctors in the department of Urology at Kasturba Hospital, Manipal. He has completed his M.B.B.S, M.S.(Gen Surgery) and MCh(Urology) from the prestigious Kasturba Medical College, Manipal under Manipal Academy of Higher Education.
Dr. Hameed is associated with the Department of Urology, KMC Manipal for the last 8 years and has vast experience in treating patients with urological conditions. He has keen interest in bladder cancer, stone disease, endourology and laparoscopic urology. He is a team person and very well admired by his peers. He has various publications in the field of urology and takes active part in urology conferences.
Dr. Hameed is also the Chief of KMC Innovation Centre and has keen interests in medical innovations. He has innovated the ‘Zedd’ scissors and ‘Rector’ scissors needle system which are used for the treatment of bladder tumors. Dr. Hameed has launched a novel smart phone application called UROSTENTZ, which is used to track the ureteric stents and stent related symptoms. He has received various awards and accolades at both national and international events.
