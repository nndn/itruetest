Background
  The i-TRUE Group came into existence in March 2020 with a vision of continuous research and learning process in the ever-growing fields of Uro-oncology and Endourology.
  i-TRUE is group of committed clinicians, researchers, technical experts and trainees, who have come together to improve patient care, by performing high quality investigative research in Endourology and Uro-oncology.
